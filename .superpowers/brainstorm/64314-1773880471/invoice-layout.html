Notes

Both layouts include the same data: company info, bill-to address, line items with SKU/variant, subtotal/shipping/tax/total breakdown, and a footer with notes. The difference is visual tone — A is dark and corporate, B is light and modern. Your email templates currently use a dark theme by default with indigo accents, so A is more visually consistent with your brand, but PDFs are typically printed — B may be more practical for that reason.
